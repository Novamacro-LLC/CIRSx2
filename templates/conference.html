Speakers

Scott McMahon, MD
Founder
Medical Expert

Dr. McMahon was the first physician certified in the Shoemaker Protocol for treatment of CIRS.  Trained by Dr. Shoemaker himself, he has also collaborated in research, authored or co-authored a dozen scientific papers and 3 medical textbook chapters, delivered more than 50 CIRS presentations at statewide, national and international medical meetings, taught Proficiency Partners, testified as an expert in 10 states and co-authored and co-edited a book on CIRS.

Greg Weatherman, CMC
Founder
Environmental Expert

Since 1997 Greg Weatherman worked with mold & bacteria, originally as a Virginia Class A contractor and asbestos abatement. He has been working with Dr. Shoemaker’s patients since 2001. Now operating from the southeast US, as a licensed Florida Mold Assessor. His company - aerobiological Solutions - and SurvivingRemediation specializes in testing of mold and bacteria from water damaged indoor environments. Greg has recently been on two IICRC standards committees (S520 Standard for Professional Mold Remediation & S700 Standard for Professional Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration), is one of the inventors of the patented air cleaning product, AeroSolver. He performs both on-site and virtual inspections world-wide.

Eric Dorninger, ND
Founder
Sponsorship & Attendee Liason

Eric Dorninger completed his Doctor of Naturopathic Medicine and Masters of Science in Acupuncture at Bastyr University in 2003 and completed a 2 year residency in Naturopathic Primary Care. In 2005, He founded Roots and Branches Integrative Health Care, where he focuses on elucidating the underlying causes of unrelenting chronic Illness.

Marcia Cash
Founder
Project Manager

Before co-founding CIRSx, Marcia held key roles in various industries, including Trans Island Airways and the U.S. Department of the Interior. She developed and patented the AeroSolver air cleaning process which has greatly improved the standard remediation methods.
