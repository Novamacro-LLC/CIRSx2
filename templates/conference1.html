X25 Keynote Speaker

Dale Bredesen, M.D.

A Unifying theory of Neurodegeneration: The Roles of Environmental Exposure

An expert in the mechanisms of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's disease. He is a graduate of Caltech, and received his MD from Duke University Medical Center. His career has included serving as Chief Resident in Neurology at the University of California, San Francisco, and served as a NIH Postdoctoral Fellow.
