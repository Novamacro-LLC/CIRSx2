X25 Keynote Speaker

A Unifying theory of Neurodegeneration: The Roles of Environmental Exposure

An expert in the mechanisms of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's disease. He is a graduate of Caltech, and received his MD from Duke University Medical Center.
