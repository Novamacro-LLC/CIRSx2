Advancing & Innovating

Research Committee

The mission of the CIRSx Research Committee is to advance the science through:

Conducting rigorous, well-designed research on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of innate immune system dysregulation caused by biotoxin exposure and dissemination of evidence-based research findings to increase biotoxin illness awareness among the general public, medical providers, lawyers, builders, and environmental professionals. 

We're in the early stages of developing this outreach program and we'll update this page with more information soon.
